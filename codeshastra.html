CODESHASTRA 7.0

The Best student chapter of CSI is back with their Flagship Event

DJCSI's CodeShastra, Mumbai's first 24 hour college level hackathon has entered in the 7th edition

Yes, we know it’s a bummer the hackathon can’t be held offline, but we won’t rob you of a superlative
hackathon that DJCSI so diligently brings to you every year.

What’s new this year?

This year round, the hackathon will be held for a total of 24 hrs, but of course, sans the exhilarating
feeling of coding with your teammates in a dimly lit room in the dark of the night!

You will be coding from the confines of your homes, but we will try to emulate the same environment that
an offline hackathon provides, to the best of our abilities.

The committee has come up with a new and exciting theme for the problem statements, which will test
your technical and innovative skills to a great extent. Esteemed stalwarts from the industry are gonna be
the mentors, and will be available throughout the course of the hackathon, ready to be of assistance
should any problem arise.

With a footfall of almost 400 programmers from all ends of the city, CodeShastra desires to bring out
best in you and bring about the best for you.

Our aim is to provide a platform for the participants as they work in synergy to devise ingenious solutions
to tackle various real life problems.




Date : 13th & 14th March, 2021.
