ABOUT

DJCSI’s initiative, Pixel Paranoia!

Do you have a knack for design? Are you a problem solver? Can you navigate through challenges? If your answer is yes, we have just the
competition for you!

“Pixel Paranoia” is an open platform for all designers to flaunt their designing skills.

This event spans over a time period of two days, and it consists of two competitions. First is Graphic Designing and the second is UI / UX
Competition, and you can participate in both.!

You will be asked to design a working prototype for your selected real-time corporate problem statement and submit your solution under
the guidance of experts in the field. Some of the domains include “Food-centric E-Commerce”, “Banking”, “Education Marketplace”, and
many more!

Participants can use Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD or any similar tool for UI / UX Competition. Similarly, there are no restrictions on tools used
in Graphic Designing.

The participants will be felicitated with fancy merchandise and goodies, while winners will get a chance to bag exciting internships!
