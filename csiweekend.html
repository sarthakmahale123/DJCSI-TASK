Tech Tac Toe

A fresh take on the
classic X and 0.

Tech - related questions will be presented
to the participating teams before each turn.
The team who answers first will get a
chance to fill the X / 0 grid. In the event of a
tie, the team with the lower overall
response time will be the winner.

atlasT

Good old atlas,
revamped.

Competing two teams should have four
players each. The game starts with a toss,
and the team that wins the toss will be given
a chance to come up with a word related to
the category assigned to them. Further, a
player from the second team should guess a
word beginning from the last letter of the
given word by the first team. The next move
is played by team one again and should
guess in the same manner. The round
continues in a similar fashion until a player
fails to come up with a word within 10
seconds. The player will be eliminated. The
team with a single player remaining loses
the game.




Roll it for century
The first team to reach a
hundred wins.

A set of 5 emojis will be provided to the
team members. The participants need to
guess the names as per the category
specified next to it. The team which
completes this first has to report first. Roll
the die until you achieve the number 6 on it.
Once you do, start writing numbers from 1 to
100. As soon as the other team rolls the die
and gets a thumbs up with number six the
other team will stop writing and roll the die
again until 6 is achieved. This will
simultaneously go on until one team finally
finishes writing till 100 and wins the game.

Lockout Debugging
Competition

Hustle to debug the bug!

A random question with a logical output
would be provided to
the competing two teams. Each Team will be asked to create
errors in the question, and give their
opponent the question. Teams will have 15
minutes to debug. The team that debugs
first gains a victory and will be qualified for
the next hustling round!
