Event 1: Workshop on Git and GitHub.

Date: 15th January 2021

For its first event of the year, DJCSI conducted a workshop on git and GitHub, a powerful tool and an important concept for
young developers to understand. The workshop, conducted online on MSTeams on 15th January 10 am onwards, was a highly
informative and concise session for introducing students to the basics of git, GitHub and version control.
