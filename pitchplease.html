DJCSI’s initiative, Pitch Please!

Have you ever got hundreds of ideas in the head at once, but not able to find the right direction to execute them and see it to fruition?

‘Pitch Please’ is an event that helps forge a path for students who need a direction and clear view to implement their
ideas as winners get to bring their ideas to life in reality.

Access to mentors at top tech companies and virtual sponsor-led panels and workspace; We aim to develop and hone the entrepreneur mind of
students and provide them with a platform to tackle real-world issues and work an approach on actual problem statements given by companies thriving
in the industry.

Compete from anywhere. Opportunity to work with other students and professionals virtually.

Having hooked onto the problem, now you want to paint a picture of what the world will be like when you solve the problem. Your proposed solution
should relate the current situation to a desired result and describe the benefits that will accrue when the desired result is achieved.

Problem statements ranging over a plethora of domains viz. Artificial Intelligence, Web/App Development, I.O.T. and Digital Marketing.

Build your skills and boost your resume. The winners would be given an opportunity to turn their ideas into reality in association with prominent
companies!
